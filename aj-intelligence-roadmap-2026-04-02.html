B

Autoreason Quality Gate

Adversarial debate loop for all client-facing copy
P1 2-3 days ↓

Interface

/autoreason <task> [--intensity full|light] [--voice <client-slug>]

The Loop

Author A Sonnet — writes initial draft (task only) ↓ Strawman Sonnet — attacks draft, problems only ↓ Author B Sonnet — rewrites addressing critique ↓ Synthesizer Sonnet — merges best of A + B ↓ Judge Panel Opus x1 (light) / x3 (full) — Blind eval, ranked choice + Borda count ↓ Winner → new A — Loop until streak = 1 (light) or 2 (full)
