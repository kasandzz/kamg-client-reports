🚨 Critical Issues

Impact: 40% of pipeline applicants cannot afford the program, wasting sales capacity and crushing morale.

Root cause: No financial pre-qualification in the quiz or application flow. The quiz qualifies on interest/fit but never addresses budget. Closers discover affordability issues only during live calls.

Fix: Add a soft income qualifier early in the quiz flow ("What best describes your annual household income?"). This single question could eliminate 40% of unqualified calls and free up 8-10 hours/week of closer time.

Impact: 84% of all lost deals die at the "Booked Lead" stage. This is not a closing problem; it is a show-rate and qualification problem.

Root cause: Weak pre-call nurture sequence. No SMS reminders. No "what to expect" content. Leads book calls with low commitment and ghost.

Fix: Implement a 3-touch pre-call sequence (confirmation + value video + day-of SMS). Target: improve show rate from ~45% to 70%+. Worth $50K-$75K/yr in recovered revenue.

Impact: Application volume has declined 81% year-over-year. The top of the XFA funnel is collapsing.

Root cause: No paid acquisition. Zero buyer-intent SEO content. Organic traffic is high-volume but low-intent (dating content). The podcast drives awareness but has no conversion bridge to XFA.

Fix: Build a professional-intent content layer (executive communication, leadership presence, high-stakes networking). Create bridge content connecting podcast audience to XFA qualification quiz.

Impact: Cannot measure what's working. No UTM tracking, broken cross-domain tracking, no event layer. Every marketing dollar is spent blind.

Root cause: No measurement strategy was ever implemented. GA4 is installed but unconfigured. No conversion events. No attribution model.

Fix: 4-phase tracking implementation (see Tracking Fixes section). Week 1 alone will provide 80% of needed visibility. Total effort: ~74 hours across 4-6 weeks.

Impact: 53% of traffic comes from Google organic, but the keywords ranking are dating/attraction topics. The XFA buyer is a 39yo professional earning $10K+/mo. These audiences have near-zero overlap.

Fix: Build 5 professional pillar pages targeting executive communication, leadership charisma, networking strategy, high-stakes conversations, and professional presence. 12-month target: 25,000 professional-intent sessions.

Impact: 72% of traffic is mobile, but the quiz and application flow are optimized for desktop. Mobile conversion rate is significantly lower.

Fix: Mobile-first redesign of the quiz flow. Reduce form fields, add progress indicators, optimize tap targets. Consider a multi-step mobile-native application flow.

Impact: Only 26.7% of quiz starters complete it. Industry benchmark is 60-75%. This means 73% of warm leads are lost mid-funnel.

Fix: Reduce quiz to 5-7 questions max. Add progress bar. Remove friction questions early. A/B test a "quick assessment" version vs current. Each 10% improvement in completion = ~$20K additional pipeline value.

Impact: Visitors who don't convert leave with zero capture. No email, no retargeting pixel fire, no exit-intent offer.

Fix: Add exit-intent popup with a lower-commitment offer (free assessment, downloadable guide, or webinar invite). Build a nurture sequence from this secondary capture to warm leads toward XFA over 14-30 days.
