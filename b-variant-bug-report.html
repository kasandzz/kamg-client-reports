What's Happening

Every CTA button on the B page ("Save My Seat Now," "Save My Seat -- Just $27," "Get VIP Access," "Save My Seat -- $27") is an <a> anchor link pointing to #section-K5FyHwqhu6. When clicked, they scroll the user down to the registration/checkout form in that section.

Likely Root Causes

  1. GoHighLevel form widget not connected -- If the B page was duplicated from A in the GHL page builder, the embedded order form inside #section-K5FyHwqhu6 may not have been reconnected. It may be rendering as an empty container.
  2. JavaScript error blocking form render -- Multiple B sessions recorded console errors. A JS error could be killing the form widget before it renders.
  3. Iframe not loading -- GHL checkout forms often render inside iframes. If the iframe src is misconfigured or broken, the form section appears blank.
  4. Section ID mismatch -- The CTAs point to #section-K5FyHwqhu6 but the actual form section on B might have a different ID, so the scroll lands on an empty area.

What to Fix

Quickest check first: Open the B page in a browser, open DevTools (F12), scroll to #section-K5FyHwqhu6, and check the console for errors and whether the form element/iframe is present in the DOM.
  • Open #section-K5FyHwqhu6 in the GHL page builder on the B page
  • Verify the order form / checkout widget is present and connected to the correct funnel/product
  • Check browser console for JS errors on the B page specifically
  • Test the full purchase flow end-to-end on the B page before re-enabling ad traffic
Expected outcome: Once fixed, B is expected to outperform A based on its stronger engagement metrics (longer sessions, lower bounce rate, fewer rage clicks overall).
