Clutch Review Questions

Click each number to mark as covered. Keep it conversational.

💡
Don't read these robotically. Have a natural conversation. Use their answers to flow into the next question. The hints tell you what information you're trying to capture.
Background ~2 min
1
Tell me about your company. What do you guys do? You're getting: company name, industry, size, what they sell/do
2
And what's your role there? You're getting: their title + responsibilities
3
What did we do for you? What was the scope of the work? You're getting: which services they hired you for
The Challenge ~2 min
4
What was the problem you were trying to solve when you came to us? You're getting: the business pain or challenge Follow-up if vague: "What was happening in the business that made you say, we need help with this?"
5
What were your goals going into it? You're getting: specific targets -- revenue, leads, traffic, etc.
The Solution ~4 min
6
How did you find us? And why'd you go with us over other options? You're getting: referral / Google / Clutch + what tipped the decision
7
Walk me through what we actually did. What did the engagement look like? You're getting: detailed description of the work, process, deliverables Follow-up: "What was the process like working together day-to-day?"
8
How big was the team on your side? And on ours? You're getting: team size + composition
9
When did we start, and is it still ongoing? You're getting: start date, duration, current status
10
Can you share the rough budget? Totally fine to keep it confidential on Clutch. You're getting: investment range -- optional but helpful If they hesitate: "No worries at all, Clutch has a confidential option for that."
Results ~5 min
11
What results have you seen? Any numbers you can share? THIS IS THE MONEY QUESTION -- get specifics: percentages, revenue, leads, traffic Dig deeper: "You mentioned a 30% increase -- do you know roughly what that was in revenue?"
12
How was the project management side? Communication, responsiveness? You're getting: feedback on working together -- meetings, updates, organization
13
What impressed you most about working with us? You're getting: the standout quote for your website/proposals
14
Anything we could've done better? Clutch expects this. Reviews with zero criticism look fake. A small thing here adds credibility.
15
What advice would you give someone thinking about working with us? You're getting: a recommendation in their words -- strongest testimonial material Alternative closer: "Anything else you'd want other business owners to know?"
