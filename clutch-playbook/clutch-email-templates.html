Email 1

The Initial Ask

Send: After a win or at 90-day mark
Subject: Quick 15-min feedback call? (I'll make it painless)
Hey [CLIENT NAME], Hope you're doing well! I'm putting together some case studies and I'd love to get your perspective on the work we've done together. Nothing formal -- just a quick 15-minute video chat where I ask you a few questions about your experience. Here's the deal: I know filling out review forms is tedious, so I'll handle all the heavy lifting. All you'd need to do is hop on a quick call, and I'll take care of the rest. Would you have 15 minutes this week or next? Happy to work around your schedule. Thanks, [YOUR NAME]
Why this works: You're framing it as "feedback" not "review." You're removing the burden ("I'll handle the heavy lifting"). And you're asking for time, not effort.
Email 2

Sending the Call Link

Send: Once they agree to the call
Subject: All set for [DAY/TIME] -- here's the link
Hey [CLIENT NAME], Looking forward to our chat on [DAY at TIME]. Here's the link to join: [RIVERSIDE STUDIO LINK] No downloads needed -- just click and it opens in your browser. Quick heads up: it'll record in high quality video so we can use some clips for a case study (with your approval, of course). The whole thing will take 15 minutes max. I'll just ask you a few questions about the project and your experience. Talk soon, [YOUR NAME]
Key detail: You mention the video recording upfront so they're not surprised. "With your approval" gives them an easy out that almost nobody takes.
Between Email 2 and 3: Do the call. Use the Questions Reference Sheet during the call. After the call, transcribe their answers, clean them up, and map to Clutch sections. Then send Email 3.
Email 3

The Copy-Paste Email

Send: Within 24-48 hrs after the call
Subject: Your Clutch review -- all pre-filled, just copy & paste
Hey [CLIENT NAME], Thanks so much for taking the time to chat -- I really appreciate the kind words! As promised, I've taken what you said on our call and formatted it to match the Clutch review questions. This way you don't have to type anything from scratch. Here's the link to leave the review: https://review.clutch.co Quick steps: 1. Click the link above 2. Sign in with your work email, Google, or LinkedIn 3. Choose "Classic Review Form" (the left option -- not the AI chat) 4. Copy and paste the answers below into the matching questions 5. Add your star ratings (takes 30 seconds) 6. Hit submit You can keep the project cost confidential if you prefer -- totally up to you. Here are your pre-filled answers: --- BACKGROUND [Paste their answer about their company, role, and what you did for them] THE CHALLENGE [Paste their answer about the problem they were solving] THE SOLUTION [Paste their answer about how they found you, what the work looked like, team size, timeline, budget] RESULTS [Paste their answer about outcomes, project management, what impressed them, what could improve, and advice for future clients] --- That's it! Should take about 5 minutes since everything's already written out. If anything doesn't sound right, feel free to tweak it -- these are your words, just cleaned up. Thanks again, [CLIENT NAME]. It means a lot. Best, [YOUR NAME]
Classic Review Form only. The AI chat option rewrites their words into generic fluff and defeats the entire purpose of our system. Always tell the client to pick "Classic Review Form" (the left option).
Email 4

The Gentle Nudge

Send: Only if no submission after 5-7 days
Subject: Re: Your Clutch review -- all pre-filled, just copy & paste
Hey [CLIENT NAME], Just checking in -- did you have any trouble with the Clutch link? Happy to walk you through it if needed. If you're swamped, no worries at all. The pre-filled answers from our call are still in my last email whenever you get a chance. Thanks! [YOUR NAME]
One nudge max. If they don't do it after this, let it go. You can always re-ask after the next win. Pestering kills the relationship.
