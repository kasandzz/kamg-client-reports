Executive summary

The three findings that should drive this week's decisions.

Finding 1 -- Therapists x Text Ad is the only 2x+ ROAS combo on the account, and it is starved of budget

Of the 25 audience x angle combinations with material spend, exactly one clears 2.0 ROAS: Therapists x Text Ad at 2.33 ROAS on $3,994 spend, 10 purchases. That is 1.8% of total account spend producing 12% of attributable revenue. "Therapists Text v5 a" alone returned 9.87 ROAS on $915. Recommendation: 3x the budget on this combination this week, with two fresh text variants in parallel.

Finding 2 -- Millionaire Alliance audience and Retargeting / Warm pools are dead-zones, $22K wasted in 30d

MA / 500K NW audience: $13,198 spend, 0.02 ROAS, 9 purchases over 30 days. Russ Castle MA Ad 1 alone burned $3,866 at 0.04 ROAS while sitting at "Below Average" quality and is still active. Retargeting / Warm: $9,004 spend across 179 ads, 0.02 ROAS, 7 purchases. The "Retargeting Ad 17 - Build Once Sell Daily" hit 2.96 frequency at 0.01 ROAS before pausing. The warm-pool pattern is structural -- either pixel/CAPI is misfiring on these audiences (likely, per Brickell/Lucah attribution issues already on file) or the warm offer fundamentally is not converting. Either way, $22K in two diagnostic-grade dead-zones in 30 days. Recommendation: pause MA entirely; rebuild ONE warm pool from scratch on Hyros 7-day attendees with 3 fresh creatives; escalate to tracking specialist if it does not lift to ~1.0 ROAS in 7 days.

Finding 3 -- Educators is the highest-ROAS niche audience at 0.49 but has zero active ads right now

Over the 30-day window, Educators / Teachers produced $22,476 spend at 0.49 ROAS and 67 purchases (CPA $335). It is the #1 niche audience by ROAS on the account. Yet zero ads are currently active in Educator-tagged adsets. Either the Educators v2 LP relaunch (Akari rules from 2026-04-14) is mid-deploy and this is intentional, or a winning audience has gone dark unintentionally. Educator Ad 4 hit 1.42 ROAS on $6,774 with Above Average engagement -- this is a creative we should be running, not pausing. Recommendation: confirm with Akari whether the gap is intentional; if not, relaunch the top Educator creative this week.

Per the established attribution architecture, only $27 ticket and $54 VIP events flow to Meta CAPI. High-ticket enrollment revenue lives in Hyros (and downstream in GHL and Stripe). The 0.34 blended ROAS reflects only the front-end ticket layer. To see real funnel ROAS, we need a Hyros + BQ join on the same window, which is queued as a follow-up once the Meta token is refreshed. Historically the multiplier from CAPI ROAS to true funnel ROAS has been roughly 8x-15x for working campaigns.
