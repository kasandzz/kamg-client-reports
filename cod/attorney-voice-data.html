What Stops Them

Top Objections Ranked

The real reasons attorneys don't close, with exact language from call notes.

1

Money / Cash Flow

Most common. Despite high hourly rates, many attorneys have tight cash flow. Affirm denials are frequent. "Down to $500 in her bank account." "In chapter 11 bankruptcy." "Recently denied a $500 cc from Wayfair."

2

Trust / Burned Before

"Spent nearly six figures on programs, never got result." Attorneys expect "pay = deliver the result" and coaching models feel foreign.

3

Timing

Busy caseloads, trials, can't start immediately. "Has some big deals he needs to close first."

4

Spouse / Partner Approval

Need to consult partner or spouse before deciding. Common in attorneys with families.

5

Review Terms First

Want to review members agreement before signing. Pure attorney instinct. "LAWYER SO MAKES SENSE."

6

"Not Ready"

Want podcast first, want more info, want to see proof before committing.
