Executive Summary

Performance Overview

COD spent $711,881 on Meta Ads over 90 days and generated $297,400 in gross revenue. Blended 0.42 ROAS -- for every dollar spent, COD got back 42 cents. ROAS dropped from 0.86 to 0.45 (48% decline period-over-period).

Cost per enrollment: $8,079 → $10,661 (up 32%). CPMs rose 19% ($61 → $73). $162K spent on a single campaign with $0 revenue.

  1. Attribution is broken (4.25x CPA discrepancy between Meta and Stripe)
  2. Campaign structure is chaotic (30 campaigns, no methodology)
  3. Retargeting is dead ($62K spent, $324 revenue, 0.005x ROAS)
  4. Spending without learning (campaigns running months with $0 revenue)
  5. One campaign carries everything (35% spend, ~100% revenue)
Bottom line: This account needs an intervention, not an optimization.
