Section 1 · Universal

Rules that apply to all three pages

Make these edits on every page. Same wording, same location.

All Pages

Section 3 · Coach Only

Coach page extras

10
Fix the split-test form URL mismatch
Coach
Before

Ads point to /registration2 but the form on this preview is wired to /hto-workshop (or vice versa). The split test is contaminated.

After · Steps

1. Open Coach preview in GHL editor.
2. Click the opt-in form → Settings → On Submit.
3. Confirm the redirect/action URL matches the URL in the Meta ad set (Kas will send the exact path).
4. Both must be the SAME path — whichever path the ad uses.

Why: Traffic from ads is landing on a page whose form submits to a different variant than the one being tested. The split-test data is unusable until this is fixed.

Therapist
