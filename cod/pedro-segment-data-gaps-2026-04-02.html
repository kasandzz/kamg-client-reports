How these numbers were calculated:

  • # Booked Calls: Unique people (by email) with at least one booking event in Feb 1 - Apr 2 range. From full 1,908-row [BOOK] API pull. De-duplicated because reschedules create multiple booking events for the same person.
  • CPBC: FB Spend [PEDRO] / Unique booked people [BOOK]. E.g. Therapists: $103,861 / 136 = $764.
  • Show Rate*: Estimated from resolved outcomes (no-shows + cancellations) in GHL booking status. GHL does NOT reliably track "Showed" (only 2 of 809 bookings). So show rate = (Resolved - NoShows) / Resolved. These are directional. The blended show rate from [DPL] + [STATS] (68.2%) is more accurate for the total.
  • Enrollments: Cross-referenced from [BCT-F] [BCT-M] [STATS] enrollment names matched to [BOOK] UTM data. Only 17 of 71 could be attributed (see Section 5).
  • CPA: FB Spend / Attributed Enrollments. INFLATED because most enrollments couldn't be attributed. Real CPA will be lower.
  • Attribution method: 154 via UTM Medium, 48 via FA-URL page path, 185 via workshop email cross-ref = 387 of 809 booking events (48%). The 52% unattributed are mostly rescheduled entries (294) and organic/direct bookings with no ad attribution.

8. Data Accuracy Caveats

FactorImpactDirection
Conversion lag [PEDRO] "We may still be closing people from this." Leads from Feb-Apr spend are still in pipeline. Sales cycle = 2-6 weeks from purchase to enrollment. Recent leads haven't had time to convert. CPA will improve over time
Hyros attribution is "scientific" [PEDRO] Pedro's purchase counts come from Hyros scientific attribution, not raw Ads Manager. Hyros may attribute differently than Meta (e.g., de-duplication, cross-device). CPP is an average that "may be skewed." Numbers may not match Ads Manager exactly
Rescheduled bookings lose UTM 42% of bookings in our 90-row sample had empty FA-UTM Medium. These are mostly reschedules. The original lead registration page (FA-URL) is preserved, so page-based attribution can recover these. Segment counts understated if using UTM only
Split-pay revenue gaps Many enrollees show $0 in Ontraport (paid via Klarna/ClarityPay/Payva). Final Stats "Cash Collected" is more accurate but still incomplete for split-pay deals in progress. Revenue per segment will increase as payments arrive
Cross-segment bleed Some people match multiple segments (e.g., Lisette Caro: UTM = Coaches Consultants, but email = clinicalconsulting = could be Therapists). Page URL is the most reliable indicator. ~5% of attributions may be wrong segment
Broad/HV campaigns 4 Feb enrollees came through "[Purchase] All Warm" or "[Purchase] Broad A+" campaigns. These are Advantage+ or HV campaigns that aren't segment-targeted. Their CPP isn't attributable to a single segment. Some purchases/enrollments will never attribute to a segment
DPL Feb data anomaly Russ Ruffino shows 2 booked / 14 completed (700% show rate) in Feb DPL. Either a data entry error or his "completed" includes calls not from his bookings. Feb totals exclude this row to be safe. Feb completed could be 192 or 206 depending on interpretation
