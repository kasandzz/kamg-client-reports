Questions

A few things worth asking.

If your question isn't here, reply to your RSVP confirmation email and we'll handle it personally.

I'm currently in Lion's Pride. Is this included in my membership?
No. This is a separate $497 in-person event, priced at-cost to cover the venue, catering, and team logistics. We've kept the price intentionally low so the room is full of people who actually wanted to be there, not people the budget pushed away.
I graduated COD a while back. Will I be lost?
No. The eight systems are stand-alone. You don't need to remember every detail of the original COD framework to install them. We'll spend the first hour grounding everyone so the whole room starts from the same line.
It's Memorial Day weekend. Can I bring family?
Family aren't in the room itself (it's working sessions, not a conference vibe), but San Diego over Memorial Day weekend is a great reason to extend the trip. Hotel block info goes out after RSVP and the venue is walking distance to the Gaslamp Quarter and the harbor.
What if I can only make one of the two days?
You'll still get value from one day, but the systems are sequenced so Day 1 sets up Day 2. We strongly recommend committing to both, and most flights in/out work cleanly with that. If something genuinely changes for you after RSVP, email us and we'll figure it out.
Will it be recorded?
No. This is in-person only, by design. The systems get installed in the room with you, not on a replay. The dollar specifics, the tactical detail, the live offer reviews. None of that gets shared outside the 100 seats.
What do I need to bring?
Laptop (mandatory, you'll be running Claude, NotebookLM, and your existing tools), 2–3 of your most recent sales-call recordings (Fathom or Zoom), and a printed or digital copy of your current offer / pricing. Everything else is optional. We provide lunch, snacks, coffee, the rooms, the screens, the tools.
What's the refund policy?
Full refund up to 14 days before the event (May 12). After that we can transfer your seat to another COD graduate or LP member if you find one, but we can't refund. The venue and catering are locked in by then.
