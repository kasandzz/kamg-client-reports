Diagnosis

Bonus Bloat Is Killing Attendance

The VIP offer has been devalued by bonus bloat. The current VIP section sells insurance and extras (Russ AI, replay access, 1-on-1 mapping, lifetime replay) rather than the live call itself. People upgrade for the free stuff and never show up to Friday. The live VIP Q&A is buried as item 2 of 5 on the feature list. "Can't Make It? You're Covered" literally gives permission to skip. The highlight box talks about replay protection, not the live experience.

What We're Selling Now

  • Russ AI access
  • Replay insurance
  • Lifetime replay access
  • 1-on-1 mapping session
Bonuses you consume at home

What Actually Converts

  • Live session with Russ
  • Personal offer review
  • Direct feedback in real-time
  • Small group Q&A
The room you show up to
The VIP call is where Russ converts. Without attendance, VIP is just an extra $27 instead of the highest-leverage touchpoint in the funnel.

No-reschedule causes refunds Low Framed as exclusive event, not punitive. Bonuses delivered regardless.
Email sequence annoys buyers Very Low 6 touches over 5 days is standard. Each adds value.
