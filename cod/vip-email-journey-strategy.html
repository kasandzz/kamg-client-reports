Why This Matters

VIP Attendance is Broken

VIP call attendance dropped to roughly 4 people. The $27 VIP upgrade is supposed to be a 3.25x revenue multiplier because Russ converts on the live call. But the current email journey kills attendance by giving away everything before Friday. VIP buyers get upsold VIP they already bought 3+ times, and receive the same aggressive strategy call push as $27 standard buyers.

4 Bonus Dumps
Replay links, Russ AI, masterclass, and prompt library all sent before Friday. Zero reason to show up.
Possible VIP Upsells to VIP Buyers
Post-workshop emails contain "upgrade to VIP" pitches. Needs verification: these may or may not be hidden for existing VIP buyers via GHL conditional content blocks.
Zero Mid-Week Prep
Nothing between purchase and day-of. No anticipation, no prep framing.
No Post-Call Sequence
The highest-value conversion moment has zero follow-up. Journey ends at the Zoom link.
Q&A Framing
Every reminder calls it a "Q&A Call" making it sound optional and skippable.
