Testimonials & Social Proof
  • Confirm [VIP_TESTIMONIAL] (Email 2) is a real quote from a named past attendee. Do not ship placeholder.
  • [VIP_TESTIMONIAL_ENROLLED] (Email 7) must be a real quote from a VIP attendee who enrolled. Pull from Fathom call recordings or direct testimonial capture. Do not fabricate.
  • Ensure [VIP_TESTIMONIAL_ENROLLED] includes standard results disclaimer per COD FTC protocol.
SMS Compliance
  • Test SMS character counts with longest expected first names (e.g. "Christopher" = 11 chars)
  • Confirm [ZOOM_LINK] short URL format for SMS. If raw Zoom link exceeds limit, use cod.link/vip redirect
Workflow Logic
  • This VIP sequence replaces/supplements the standard workshop reminder flow for VIP purchasers. VIP contacts should receive this sequence instead of (not in addition to) the generic workshop reminders. Ensure GHL workflow branches on VIP tag.
  • Email 9 is trigger-based. Only fires if contact opened at least one of Emails 6-8 without clicking the strategy call link. Set this condition in GHL workflow.
  • After Email 8, if no booking: move contact to general updates list
Copy & Compliance Rules
  • "Russ AI" is the correct product name throughout. Not "Delphi AI."
  • FTC: no income claims, no earnings representations, no guaranteed results language anywhere in this sequence
  • No income claims or earnings projections in any variant
