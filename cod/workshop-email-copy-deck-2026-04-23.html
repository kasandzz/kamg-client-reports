Workflow Notes for GHL Implementation

  1. VIP exit condition: After the 15min pre-workshop reminders, add condition: IF "auto-workshop-vip" tag -> END. This is where VIP buyers leave and enter their own VIP pre-event flow.
  2. 2-minute delay: If a buyer purchases standard + VIP simultaneously, add a 2-min delay on the standard confirmation email to prevent collision with VIP confirmation.
  3. "call-discovery-booked" gate: Emails 5-8 all check for this tag. If contact books a strategy call at any point, they exit the nurture sequence.
  4. Email 8 conditional: Only fires if the contact opened Emails 5, 6, or 7 but did NOT click the strategy call link. This prevents sending to completely disengaged contacts.
  5. Post-workshop timing: All post-workshop email timing is relative to workshop end time, not purchase time. This keeps the cadence aligned with the buyer's actual experience.
