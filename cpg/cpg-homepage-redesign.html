The Problem

Why Most Affiliate Programs Fail

Coupon Parasites

Agencies stuff your program with coupon sites. They get credit for sales you would have made anyway. Your CPA looks great on paper. Your customer acquisition is zero.

Purple told me to drop from $50 CPA to $25... it was all coupon traffic. -- DTC Brand Founder
Repeat Customer ROAS Inflation

Agencies chase high ROAS by driving repeat customers, not new ones. Your numbers look great. Your growth doesn't. You're paying a commission to re-acquire people already in your database.

If you chase too-high ROAS, they start cheating by driving repeat customers. -- Industry Veteran
Zero Accountability

Retainer agencies get paid whether you grow or not. Month after month. There's no incentive to perform because the invoice goes out regardless of the results.

It sounded good, but maybe too good to be true. -- Skeptical Prospect
