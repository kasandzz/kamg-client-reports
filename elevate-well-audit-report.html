Executive Summary

The Situation, The Problem,
and The Path Forward

Situation

The Ads Are Working

Elevate Well's Facebook ads are delivering high-CTR clicks to a webinar registration page. The creative resonates. The audience is engaged and clicking. The top of the funnel is not the problem.

Complication

The Page Is Destroying the Investment

The registration page converts near 0% — 3 registrations from hundreds of clicks. Industry benchmark for cold FB traffic to webinar reg is 30%. Three root causes: masculine template signals "wrong audience" in 50ms, zero social proof on a page asking strangers for personal data, and a generic CTA that kills momentum.

Resolution

A Complete Rebuild, Backed by Evidence

A complete page rebuild addressing all three root causes — backed by 85+ CRO findings from our Knowledge Base — is projected to bring conversion rate to the 20–35% range, representing a 800–1,000% lift from the current baseline.
