Your Pre-Call Homework

3 Things to Do Before Your Call

Women who come prepared get dramatically more value from this conversation. This takes about 20 minutes total.

1

Write Down Your "Enough Is Enough" Moment

What finally made you take this step? Write it down in a journal or notes app. Be specific: the morning you couldn't button your jeans, the vacation photo you avoided sharing, the energy crash at 2pm that felt like failure.

Why this matters: When you can articulate your real "why," Michelle can build a plan around it. This is the difference between generic advice and a roadmap that actually fits your life.

2

Track What You Eat and When for 2 Days

No judgment, no calorie counting. Just notice: what time you wake up and eat, what you reach for when stressed, when energy crashes happen, how often you skip meals.

Why this matters: Hormonal imbalance often shows up in patterns, not just food choices. Two days of honest observation gives Michelle something concrete to work with immediately.

3

Write Your 3 Biggest Frustrations Right Now

Examples: "I eat clean but can't lose weight," "I'm exhausted no matter how much I sleep," "I've tried everything and nothing sticks," "I don't recognize my own body anymore."

Why this matters: Michelle will spend the first part of your call understanding exactly where you are. Coming in with clarity cuts through the small talk and gets to real answers, faster.

What Actually Happens on Your Call

  • Minutes 1-10: Michelle listens. She asks about your history, what you've tried, and where you're stuck. No rushing, no scripts.
  • Minutes 10-25: Together you identify your specific hormonal patterns and what's actually blocking your progress. This alone is often worth the call.
  • Minutes 25-35: Michelle walks you through what a personalized reset plan looks like for your body, your schedule, and your life.
  • Minutes 35-40: If Michelle's Signature Transformation Program is a fit, she'll explain what working together looks like. Zero pressure. You decide on your own terms.

Is this just a sales call?

Honestly: the last 5 minutes is where Michelle shares how she can help you further if you want that. But the first 35 minutes are a real assessment, not a pitch. Women leave this call with clarity they didn't have before, whether they work with Michelle or not. That's the point.
