Before Your Call

Your Pre-Call Assignment

Complete this before your call and you'll get 3x more value from the conversation.

1

Write Down Your Top 3

What are the 3 specific things about your health that keep you up at night? Be specific.

"Low energy" is not specific.

"I crash at 2 PM every day and need 3 coffees to make it to 5" is specific.

Bring these to your call.

2

Know Your Numbers

When was your last comprehensive blood panel?

If you've had one in the past 12 months, have it ready to reference.

If you haven't, that tells us something important too.

3

Set Your 12-Week Target

If we could fix ONE thing about your health in 12 weeks, what would it be?

Weight? Energy? Testosterone? Sleep? Mental clarity?

Pick one. We'll build your plan around it.
