Section 05

The Conversion Gap

This is the primary area of focus for the next 30 days.

Your ads are doing their job -- getting clicks from qualified buyers. The gap is not the ads. It is what happens after the click. Conversion rate on the current landing page and form is the constraint, not ad performance.

What Competitors Do What Redstone Has Now Impact on Conversion
Service-specific landing pages (Xometry operates 200+ pages) Existing service pages are not optimized for ad conversion -- no specs, no file upload, no keyword-matched headlines Competitors build dedicated pages per keyword with specs, upload forms, and matched headlines. Pages built for ad traffic convert 2-3x higher than general service pages.
Instant quote with CAD / STEP / IGES file upload "Get Free Quote" links to a basic contact form Engineers expect to upload files and receive pricing. A contact form creates friction that loses an estimated 50-70% of visitors at this stage.
Published technical specs: tolerances, materials, max dimensions General capabilities described in paragraph format Engineers cannot self-qualify against specs. They leave to check a competitor that publishes the data they need to make a decision.
Multiple entry points: Quote + Talk to Engineer + Download Spec Sheet Single contact method for all visitor types Buyers at different stages of the decision process need different entry points. A single form does not match the range of intent.
