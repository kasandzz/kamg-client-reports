Key Recommendations

1. Bidding Strategy Transition Plan

Weeks 1-3: Manual CPC (Enhanced) across all campaigns. Collect conversion data.

Week 4+: Once you hit 15+ conversions in Campaign 1, switch to Maximize Conversions (no target CPA yet).

Month 2+: Once you hit 30+ conversions, set a target CPA of 1.5x your current average CPA. Tighten 10% per month.

2. Match Type Strategy

Week 1-4: Exact + Phrase only. No broad match until you have 50+ search terms to review.

Month 2+: Add Broad match on top-performing keywords only, paired with smart bidding. Broad match without smart bidding = wasted spend.

Always: Review search terms every 3 days in Month 1, weekly after that. Add negatives aggressively.

3. Ad Schedule & Geo Targeting

Schedule: Mon-Fri, 7am-9pm EST (B2B decision makers). Pause weekends initially to conserve budget.

Geo: United States only. Consider adding Canada and UK in Phase 2 if budget allows.

Device: Desktop 70%, Mobile 30% bid adjustment. B2B SaaS/agency searches skew desktop during work hours.

4. Landing Page Requirements

Each campaign needs a dedicated landing page -- do NOT send traffic to the homepage.

  • Campaign 1 LP: "Performance Affiliate Marketing for Ecommerce Brands" -- hero with no-retainer value prop, social proof, 3-step process, book-a-call CTA
  • Campaign 2 LP: Platform/vertical-specific variants (Shopify-focused, DTC-focused) with the same core template
  • Must-haves: Case study numbers, "No Retainer" messaging above fold, Calendly/call booking widget, mobile-optimized

5. Conversion Tracking Setup

Primary Conversion: Discovery call booked (Calendly thank-you page or form submission confirmation)

Secondary Conversions: Phone calls (30+ seconds), contact form submissions, chat initiations

Enhanced Conversions: Enable from Day 1. Upload offline conversion data (call outcomes) monthly for smart bidding optimization.
