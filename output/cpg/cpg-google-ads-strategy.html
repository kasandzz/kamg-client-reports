NEVER launch a new account on Maximize Conversions with zero conversion history. Google has no data to optimize against and will burn budget on junk clicks.

What CPG Does

Performance-based affiliate marketing agency for DTC e-commerce brands. Sits between brands and publishers (traffic sources), recruiting publishers to drive sales on a CPA (cost-per-acquisition) model.

  • Model: 100% CPA-based. Brands pay only on completed sales.
  • Setup: One-time $3K fee + $5K CPA budget in escrow
  • Contract: Day-to-day agreements, no locked-in periods, 10-day exit

  • Problem section is unique. No competitor addresses coupon fraud, false ROAS, or retainer misalignment on their LP. CPG names the enemy.

Pre-Launch Fixes Required

  • Form submission dead. No backend, no webhook, no CRM endpoint. Must wire up before going live.

  • Publisher logos are CSS wordmarks, not real brand marks. Sophisticated prospects may notice.
  • Scarcity claim hardcoded. "Only 3 spots" is static HTML. Either make dynamic or remove.
  • No video. A 60-second Krik explainer would dramatically lift engagement for high-ticket B2B.

04 Campaign Architecture: BOFU-First, LP-Per-Cluster

$5K/month budget. BOFU keywords only at launch — leads are the priority. Each keyword cluster gets a dedicated landing page. Every ad group points to a hyper-relevant LP that speaks directly to that searcher's intent.

Strategic rationale: With $5K/mo and a lead-gen goal, we skip informational/TOFU entirely. Every dollar goes to searchers actively looking to hire, outsource, or buy affiliate management services. LP-per-cluster means message match is 1:1 — the headline on the LP mirrors the search query. This compounds Quality Score, lowers CPC, and lifts conversion rate simultaneously.

Why LP-Per-Cluster Wins

Message Match

Searcher for "supplement brand affiliate marketing" lands on a page that says "Affiliate Marketing for Supplement Brands" in the H1. 1:1 match = higher Quality Score = lower CPC = higher CTR.

Relevant Social Proof

Supplement LP features SuperMush, Everyday Dose, MTN OPS testimonials. Beauty LP features relevant beauty brands. Each LP tells the searcher "we've done this for brands like yours."

Quality Score Compounding

Google rewards LP relevance in Quality Score (1-10). Higher QS = lower CPC at the same ad position. Over 3 months, this can reduce CPC 20-40% vs. a generic LP, effectively giving you more budget.
