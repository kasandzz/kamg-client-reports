Section 12

Strategic Recommendations

Priority actions for Redstone to gain competitive advantage in Google Ads.

P0 -- Immediate

1. Build Service-Specific Landing Pages

Create dedicated pages for: Die Casting, CNC Machining, Sheet Metal, Investment Casting, Sand Casting. Each page needs: headline matching the ad keyword, technical specs (tolerances, materials, max dimensions), certifications, client logos, and a quote form with file upload. This is what every competitor does. Without it, Google Ads traffic bounces off the homepage.

P0 -- Immediate

2. Launch Nearshore/Mexico Campaign

Zero competitors are bidding on "nearshore manufacturing," "Mexico manufacturing," or "manufacturing outsourcing Mexico." This is a wide-open lane with low CPCs ($2-5). Build a dedicated landing page around "The trustworthiness of an American company with the pricing from overseas." Target US + Canada. Rachel's hook IS the ad.

P0 -- Immediate

3. Add Quote Form with File Upload

Every major competitor accepts CAD file uploads. At minimum, add a quote form with: company name, email, phone, service needed (dropdown), file upload (STEP/IGES/PDF), quantity, and notes. This is the bridge until a full instant quote system is built. Put it on every service page.

P0 -- Immediate

4. Implement Remarketing

Install Google Ads tag on redstonemanufacturing.com. Create remarketing audiences for: all site visitors, service page visitors, quote page visitors. Run Display remarketing ads ($300-500/month) to stay top of mind during the long B2B sales cycle. This is the highest-ROI campaign type at the lowest budget.

P1 -- Next 30 Days

5. Build Competitor Comparison Pages

Fictiv and RapidDirect rank for "[competitor] alternative" keywords with zero ad spend. Build: "Xometry vs Redstone," "Why Choose a Direct Manufacturer vs Marketplace," "Protolabs Alternative for Die Casting." Position Redstone as the direct, US-quality alternative to impersonal marketplaces.

P1 -- Next 30 Days

6. Add Live Chat

Xometry, Protolabs, and SendCutSend all have live chat. Engineers researching parts want instant answers. Even a simple Tawk.to (free) or Intercom widget captures visitors who won't fill out a form. Set up during business hours with Rachel or an engineer on the other end.

P1 -- Next 30 Days

7. Display Phone Number Prominently

Most competitors hide their phone number. B2B manufacturing buyers, especially procurement teams at established companies, want to talk to a human. Put the phone number in the header of every page + use Call Extensions in Google Ads. This is a differentiator against faceless marketplaces.

P2 -- Next 60 Days

8. Create Material-Specific Landing Pages

Build 10-15 pages targeting specific materials: aluminum die casting, zinc die casting, stainless steel CNC, etc. Each page becomes a Google Ads landing page for material-specific keyword ad groups. Xometry has 100+ of these. Redstone needs 10-15 to start competing on long-tail.
