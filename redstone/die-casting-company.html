Core Processes

Die Casting Built for Production Volume

We run three core die casting processes across our partner foundries. Every program is quoted against your annual volume, not a one-time run.

High-Pressure Die Casting

Aluminum, zinc, and magnesium parts on 250 to 1,600 ton machines. Best for thin walls, tight repeatability, and high-cavitation tooling.

Gravity Die Casting

Permanent mold aluminum casting for thicker sections and higher mechanical properties. Used where pressure porosity is unacceptable.

Squeeze and Semi-Solid Casting

Structural aluminum components that require heat treatment or weldability. Lower porosity than standard high-pressure die casting.

Global Manufacturing · One Contract

Four Countries. One Point of Contact.

Redstone qualifies and manages die casting production at certified foundries in Mexico, India, Brazil, and China. About 80% of our buyers manufacture in Mexico because it is the shortest landed-cost path for North American OEMs, and because the IATF 16949 foundries there are used to running automotive-grade programs.

You do not manage four suppliers in four time zones. You work with one Redstone program manager in the US, who runs the DFM review, the PPAP package, the inspection plan, and the logistics. Every foundry in the network holds ISO 9001 certification at minimum, and any automotive program is routed only to IATF 16949 certified partners.

FAQ

Production & Manufacturing FAQs

What is the lead time on a new die casting program?

Tooling typically runs 6 to 10 weeks depending on complexity and cavitation. First production shipment follows 2 to 4 weeks after tool approval. Repeat orders run on agreed lead times set in your supply agreement, usually 4 to 8 weeks.

What is your minimum order?

Our partnerships start at $50,000 per order. Most of our buyers run annual volumes well above that on repeat schedules.

How is pricing handled?

One all-inclusive number per part, including tariffs, duties, freight, and 3rd party inspection. You compare it directly to your landed-cost target.

Do you do engineering or part design?

No. We manufacture to the drawings you supply. We will run a DFM review and flag casting issues in writing before tooling is cut, but the design ownership stays with your team.

What quality standards do you hold?

ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 Certified Foundries Only. PPAP Level III, FAI, and documented inspection plans are standard on production programs.

Where are parts manufactured?

Mexico, India, Brazil, and China. About 80% of our buyers choose Mexico for North American programs. You choose the country during the quote step.

What materials do you cast?

Aluminum A380, A383, A360, ADC12. Zinc ZA-8, ZA-27, Zamak 3 and 5. Magnesium AZ91D and AM60B. Brass and bronze on request.

How is production managed across four countries?

One Redstone program manager in the US owns your account. They run the foundry, the inspection partner, and the freight forwarder on your behalf. You place one PO and receive one invoice per shipment.
