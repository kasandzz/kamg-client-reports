Systemic Issues (Template-Level)

These affect multiple pages from shared templates. One fix per issue propagates across all affected pages.

P0 #1 — Plural "manufacturers" / "foundries" in FAQ & headings

Violation: "Redstone's certified manufacturers support aluminum..." and "Automotive-Grade Certified Foundries"

Rule: Eric said "I want users to think we are a factory in Mexico" — must feel like ONE facility.

Affected: ~18 pages (CNC FAQ template + die casting "Why Redstone" heading)

Fix: "Redstone's certified manufacturers" → "Redstone's Monterrey facility" or "our ISO 9001 certified factory". "Foundries" → "Foundry".

P0 #2 — Welding references on ALL 7 sheet metal pages

Violations found in 4 locations per page:

  • Stats bar: D1.1 / D1.2 procedures (these ARE AWS welding codes)
  • Capabilities: MIG/TIG/spot joining (MIG & TIG are welding processes)
  • Process Step 04: Weld inspection against acceptance criteria
  • FAQ certs: D1.1, D1.2, or D17.1 on 2 Mexico pages

Rule: Eric said: "Please remove AWS certified welders on all pages. We don't actually offer that."

Fix: Remove all D1.1/D1.2/D17.1 references, replace "weld inspection" with "joint inspection" or "assembly inspection", replace "MIG/TIG" with "precision joining" or remove.

BUG #3 — Live editorial notes visible to visitors

Violation: (TBD: confirm with client) appears in the stats bar on 5+ sheet metal pages.

Impact: Visitors can see unresolved draft notes. Destroys credibility.

BUG #4 — Broken/mangled FAQ text on all 7 sheet metal pages

Examples:

  • "Redstone's s hold current quality qualifications" (missing word)
  • "Redstone's [facilities] hold current quality qualifications" (unfilled placeholder)
  • "Redstone's run through holding current" (completely garbled)

Impact: Broken placeholder text live on production pages. Also contains banned word "qualifications".

P0 #5 — "qualifications" in sheet metal FAQ answers

Violation: "hold current quality qualifications" in the certifications FAQ on all 7 sheet metal pages.

Rule: Eric said the qualifications section "screens people out after they already clicked on our ad."

Fix: Rewrite FAQ cert answer to remove "qualifications" entirely. Likely gets fixed when the broken FAQ text (issue #4) is rewritten.

P0 #6 — "can stand behind a weld" copy

Page: /sheet-metal-fabrication-company/ — Why Redstone #01

Violation: "can stand behind a weld during a customer audit"

Fix: Remove or rewrite without "weld".

P0 #7 — "Independent Inspection" on lp-die-casting

Page: /lp-die-casting-services-mexico.html — Why Redstone #03 heading

Violation: "Certified Production, Independent Inspection"

Rule: Eric said no "3rd party" or "independent" QC — they do QC themselves.
