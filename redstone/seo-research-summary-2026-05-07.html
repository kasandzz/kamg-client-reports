Eight assumptions Jack made flag any that are wrong, plan adjusts

Why this matters. The plan only holds if these are true. If any are aspirational rather than real, the page strategy needs to flex. This is the most important section to walk through with Jack live.
# Assumption If false
1Existing material guides drive ~2K organic sessions/mo and will be 301'd to /resources/material-guides/, not killed.Critical, traffic loss risk. Audit before migration.
2Mexico facility is staffed + operational with documented capacity for die casting, CNC, foundry.Mexico hub messaging changes from "our facility" to "partner network". Conversion impact.
3India + China have a real QC oversight model (on-site inspections, audits).Geo pages get caveat language or get delayed until model is documented.
4Multi-step quote form is feasible with CAD upload, volume filter, tariff-inclusive estimates.Simplify to single-step or email-based RFQ. Conversion impact.
5No regulatory or export-control constraints on materials or processes we list.Specific service or geo pages need caveats.
6Halliburton case study has client permission for public disclosure ($1.2M, 50 foundries).Substitute with another case study or use anonymized testimonials.
7Domain authority is strong enough to push high-KD terms (cnc machining services KD 63, steel fabrication KD 58) to top 10 within 6-12 months.Extend timeline expectations, focus harder on KD 0-30 terms in P0/P1.
8AI Overview presence is verified on the 35 keywords flagged, as of May 6.Comparison/FAQ strategy may shift if Google's AI Overview rollout changes.
