05 / Content Calendar

90-Day Implementation Roadmap

Four phases, each building on the last. Phase 1 is pure quick-wins with zero new pages needed. Phase 2 launches the structural pages. Phase 3 starts the content engine. Phase 4 builds the moat.

1
Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
Quick Wins: Zero New Pages Required

Everything in this phase can be done on existing pages. No content creation needed. These changes will have immediate impact on crawlability, CTR, and trust signals.

  • P0 Meta descriptions on all existing pages (see templates in Section 6)
  • P0 CTA language audit: Replace all "Contact Us" with "Get Your Custom Quote"
  • P0 Logo/cert bar above fold on every page: ISO9001, IATF16949, NADCA logos
  • P0 Schema markup: Organization + LocalBusiness on homepage, BreadcrumbList sitewide
  • P1 Fat footer: 30-40 internal links across 4 columns (Services, Industries, Company, Resources)
  • P1 Robots.txt cleanup: Ensure PPC LPs on Vercel subdomain are properly noindexed
  • P1 XML sitemap: Generate clean sitemap excluding PPC pages, submit to GSC
  • P1 Homepage H1 optimization: Currently all-caps and too generic; rewrite with primary keywords
  • P2 Thomasnet profile: Claim and optimize listing with all services + certifications
  • P2 Google Business Profile: Optimize with services, photos, and regular posts
2
Phase 2: Structure (Weeks 2-4)
Service Hubs + Industry Pages + Geo Pages

Build the structural backbone of the site. These are the pages that everything else links to. Each hub page is 2,000-2,500 words with FAQ schema.

  • P0 6 Service Hub pages: /die-casting, /cnc-machining, /sheet-metal-fabrication, /precision-machining, /investment-casting, /sand-casting
  • P0 5 Industry Vertical pages: /automotive-manufacturing, /medical-device-manufacturing, /aerospace-manufacturing, /consumer-electronics-manufacturing, /industrial-manufacturing
  • P1 3 Geographic pages: /manufacturing-in-mexico, /nearshore-manufacturing, /usmca-manufacturing
  • P1 3 Certification pages: /iso-9001-certified, /iatf-16949-certified, /nadca-member
  • P1 /how-we-work page: 3-step process transparency page with visual flow
  • P1 Internal linking: Wire all new pages into hub-spoke + cross-cluster links
  • P1 Schema on all new pages: Product schema on hubs, FAQ schema on all, BreadcrumbList
3
Phase 3: Content Engine (Month 2)
Blog Launch + Gated Resources + Case Studies

Start publishing spoke content. Target 6-8 articles in Month 2. Launch gated resources for email capture. Build first case studies with real metrics from Eric's team.

  • P1 /blog launch: Blog index page + category taxonomy (by service hub)
  • P1 First 6-8 blog articles: Prioritize highest-volume spokes from each hub
  • P1 Recommended first articles:
  • 1. Aluminum Die Casting Guide (Hub 1 spoke)
  • 2. Die Casting in Mexico: USMCA Benefits (Hub 1 spoke)
  • 3. CNC Machining in Mexico (Hub 2 spoke)
  • 4. CNC Machining Cost Factors (Hub 2 spoke)
  • 5. Sheet Metal Fabrication Processes (Hub 3 spoke)
  • 6. Die Casting vs. Investment Casting (Hub 1 spoke, cross-links Hub 5)
  • P1 2 Gated resources: Nearshore Manufacturing Guide + Die Casting Material Guide
  • P2 2 Case studies: Automotive + Medical (metrics-first: cost savings %, lead time reduction)
  • P2 2 Geographic pages: /manufacturing-in-india, /china-alternative-manufacturing
4
Phase 4: Moat Building (Month 3+)
Material Library + Calculator + Video + Scale

Build the content moat. Material library is programmatic SEO targeting hundreds of long-tail material + process queries. Interactive calculator is a link magnet. Video content for plant tours and process walkthroughs.

  • P1 Material Library (programmatic): Template-driven pages for 8+ materials with specs, compatible processes, and applications
  • P1 Continue blog cadence: 6-8 articles/month targeting remaining spokes
  • P2 Interactive cost calculator: Simple form-based estimator (material + process + volume + location = ballpark)
  • P2 Video content: Plant tour videos, process walkthroughs, embed on service hubs
  • P2 Tariff and trade tracker page: Living page with USMCA updates, tariff news
  • P2 2 More gated resources: Manufacturing Cost Comparison + Quality Certification Checklist
  • P2 2 More case studies: Aerospace + Consumer Electronics
  • P3 "Best of" comparison content: "Top Die Casting Companies" style articles for SERP feature capture
  • P3 /resources index page aggregating all guides, calculators, and downloads

Internal Linking Rules

Mandatory Links

  • 1
    Every spoke article links to its parent hub within the first 200 words
  • 2
    Every hub links to all its spoke articles in a "Related Articles" section at bottom
  • 3
    Every page includes breadcrumb navigation: Home > [Type] > Page
  • 4
    Fat footer on every page: 30-40 links across 4 columns
  • 5
    Every industry page links to at least 3 service hubs
  • 6
    Geo pages link to all services offered at that location

Cross-Cluster Links

  • A
    Comparison articles link to both compared service hubs
  • B
    Material pages link to all processes that use that material
  • C
    Case studies link to relevant service hub + industry page
  • D
    Certification pages link from every page that mentions the cert
  • E
    Contextual inline links use natural anchor text (not "click here")
  • F
    Each hub has 2-3 cross-links to other hubs where processes overlap
